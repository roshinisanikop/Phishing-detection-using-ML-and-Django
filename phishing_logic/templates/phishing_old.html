Phishing Detection

Phishing is the most common technique used by hackers to access sensitive information about you. This could be your passwords, banking information, or card details. Potential victims might receive a tailored, expertly designed email which looks just like it’s from a trusted source. You’ll be directed to a suspicious website that looks just like one you normally use, such as PayPal or their online bank. Once there, the hackers hope you’ll enter your sensitive information for the hackers to steal and use it for their own gain. That’s why it’s so important to know how to tell whether a website is legit. That way, if you do find yourself on a dodgy website, you’ll be able to spot the signs and protect yourself from becoming a victim.
